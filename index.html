Digital Arts Club

Its a calm and friendly working environment where everyone always has a say, and everyone is welcomed. We are always given fun assignments and are never bored, because there is always more to do. We get great opportunities to showcase our artwork, and improve our artistic abilities.

When do they meet?

They meet every Tuesday after school from the final bell until approximately 4:30.


What do they do?

We meet to express our creative talents and work in a group setting with people who understand the process of Digital Editing.
